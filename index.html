Code Gala

Bringing like minded people in a place to explore ideas and share knowledge.

What CodeGala is ...

Its action... coding is the main aim.

What CodeGala is not ...

Its not talking. Its not the day for discussion as group. Though there might be people who talk about their ideas and plans, you cannot expect any class or session in this event.

Why do you need CodeGala ?

Explore the possibilities to use the community effectively to brainstorm innovative ideas that has good motive/impact to the society and to share knowledge

What do you benefit from CodeGala ?

Its all about Explore, Educate, Innovate, Fun ...

Who can attend CodeGala?

  • Anyone who..
  • have plan to work with.
  • project in mind,
  • who like to contribute to their favorite framework/tools etc
  • ... in general.. professionals, students or passionate java enthusiasts

Are there any prerequisites for CodeGala ?

Nothing unless you have anything in your mind :) Come up with an idea to work with, or have something in mind to learn

What is the schedule of CG ?

We meet most of the Saturday and it goes from 10am to 5pm.
